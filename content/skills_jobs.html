Level Designer


CodeCats are looking for a passionate level designer to work on you new 2D platformer game!

Every game project requires a skilled level designer to translate the game designer’s vision to the game world. Level designers’ major goal is to communicate to the players through the game environment, and ensure that the game world flows smoothly to maximize player immersion. As a level designer you will work closely with the game designer, the UX/UI designer, and narrative designer to create a player experience that intuitively blends game mechanics with the game narrative.


CodeCats are looking for an experienced level designer to round out our team for this project.

To be a successful candidate for this role it is necessary for the you to be a creative thinker with the ability to solve problems in a unique fashion. You must also be an effective storyteller with a good understanding of how players will interpret the environment and interact with the stimuli presented in the game world.

A breakdown of the role, skills, and tools you will need experience with is show below:


Role:

  • Creating levels that function within the rules laid out by the game designer.
  • Designs the basic structure of the game.
  • Refines the level to suit gameplay.
  • Has a development process that focuses on combining inter-level consistency while also thinking about user experience.
  • Interpret the original concept from the game designer.
  • Keep track of documents listing the assets, objectives, and layout of each level.
  • Sketch/map every level with highlighted action points.
  • Render the level to a 3D environment.


Tools:

  • Adobe Create Suite
  • Maya, unity, unreal
  • C, C++


Skills:

  • Creative skills.
  • Storytelling skills.
  • Understanding of physics.
  • Understanding of architecture.
  • Understanding of gameplay and how players will engage with the game world.
  • Programming.
  • Problem solving.
